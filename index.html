About Me

I am an associate professor at Oakland University, where I have taught courses in Mathematics and Computer Science. Before coming to Oakland I finished my PhD in ACO at Georgia Tech and worked as a post-doc at Zuse Institute Berlin. You can find me on Linkedin here.

Research

My research interests include optimization, computer algebra, symbolic computation, graph theory and computational methods. A primary focus of my work has been developing algorithms for finding numerically exact solutions to optimization problems.
